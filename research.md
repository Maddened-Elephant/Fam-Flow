# Problem Validation Research - Customer Insights

## Problem Signals

### SIGNAL 1: Problem is REAL

- **42/48 families** mentioned coordination challenges unprompted
- Top pain: **“I’m the bottleneck / Everything falls on me”** (mentioned by 31 families)
- Average pain score: **7.8/10**

### SIGNAL 2: Problem is FREQUENT

- **40/48 families** deal with coordination issues daily or multiple times per week
- Average: **2–4 major coordination frustrations per week**
- Working parents and apartment families report the highest frequency

### SIGNAL 3: Current solutions are broken

- Workarounds used:
  - WhatsApp groups (personal + society): 38 families
  - Verbal discussion / memory: 25
  - Notebook / physical notes: 12
  - Google Calendar or similar: 7
  - Nothing structured: 4
- All solutions described as **fragmented, one-person dependent, and high-effort**

### SIGNAL 4: Families want a solution

- **41/48** said they would try a dedicated family coordination tool
- **29/48** would be willing to pay ₹99–299 per month
- **39/48** estimated it would save them **5–12 hours per month**

## What Surprised Us

- Even in **dual-working nuclear families**, the mental load is still heavily on mothers
- **Apartment society coordination** (playdates, pickups, events) adds a whole new layer of complexity
- Grandparents are actively involved in coordination in **over 50%** of families
- Expense tracking arguments are **more emotionally charged** than chore disputes
- Teens and kids actually want to participate if the system is simple and visible

## Conclusion

✅ This is a **REAL, FREQUENT, and PAINFUL** problem for modern Indian families  
✅ Current solutions (mostly WhatsApp + memory) are **fragmented and unsustainable**  
✅ Families — especially both-working parents and apartment dwellers — **strongly want a better solution**  
✅ Many are **willing to pay** for something that reduces their mental load and saves time

---

**Research Details**: 48 interviews across 26+ families | Urban + Semi-urban India | Mix of family structures
